api.edit_story(business_connection_id, story_id, content, opts)
edit a story previously posted by the bot on behalf of a managed business account.

Parameters:

  • business_connection_id string unique identifier of the business connection
  • story_id number unique identifier of the story to edit
  • content table|string a JSON-serialized InputStoryContent describing the story content
  • opts table optional parameters
    • caption string caption of the story; 0-2048 characters after entities parsing
    • parse_mode string mode for parsing entities in the story caption
    • caption_entities table|string a JSON-serialized list of special entities in the caption
    • areas table|string a JSON-serialized list of clickable areas to be shown on the story

Returns:

  1. table|false the edited story, or false on failure
  2. string|table the HTTP status or error details
api.post_story(business_connection_id, content, active_period, opts)
post a story on behalf of a managed business account.

Parameters:

  • business_connection_id string unique identifier of the business connection
  • content table|string a JSON-serialized InputStoryContent describing the story content
  • active_period number period after which the story is moved to the archive, in seconds
  • opts table optional parameters
    • caption string caption of the story; 0-2048 characters after entities parsing
    • parse_mode string mode for parsing entities in the story caption
    • caption_entities table|string a JSON-serialized list of special entities in the caption
    • areas table|string a JSON-serialized list of clickable areas to be shown on the story
    • post_to_chat_page boolean pass true to keep the story accessible after it expires
    • protect_content boolean pass true to protect the story from forwarding and screenshotting

Returns:

  1. table|false the posted story, or false on failure
  2. string|table the HTTP status or error details
